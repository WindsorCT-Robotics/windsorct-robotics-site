FIRST®

FIRST®, or For Inspiration and Recognition of Science and Technology, is a global non-profit organization dedicated to preparing young people for the future through a suite of inclusive, team-based robotics programs. Team Paragon Robotics, Inc. participates in some of these programs to offer robotics opportunities to students in grades 4 to 12.

  • Learn more about FIRST®

Team Paragon Robotics, Inc.

Team Paragon Robotics, Inc. began as a single robotics team from The Loomis Chaffee School in Windsor, Connecticut in 2001: Team Paragon. Over the last 23 years, this has grown to an established 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization with four robotics teams consisting of over 50 students and 20 adult volunteers from around Windsor and beyond.

  • Learn more about the history of our organization

Our Programs

Team Paragon has four robotics teams participating in three FIRST programs. We are proud to support students in grades 4-12 in learning about robotics and engineering in a safe environment.

FIRST Lego League (FLL)

Our two FLL teams: Team 55814, SHARP, and Team 60884, Scissors, participate in the Challenge league for students in grades 4 to 8.

    • FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC)
